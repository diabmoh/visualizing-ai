Sources and simplifications

This exhibit compresses modern text and sentence embeddings into a deterministic visual story. All text examples, vectors, projected coordinates, similarity scores, rankings, and model behavior are illustrative rather than outputs from a live model.

Real representations may be token-level, contextual, sentence-level, document-level, or multimodal. Their behavior depends on architecture, training data, objective, pooling, normalization, language, domain, and evaluation task. Implementations vary.

  • Efficient Estimation of Word Representations in Vector Space for Word2Vec
  • GloVe: Global Vectors for Word Representation for learned word-vector geometry
  • BERT for contextual language representations
  • Sentence-BERT for semantically meaningful sentence embeddings
  • SimCSE for contrastive sentence representation learning
  • MTEB for broad, task-specific embedding evaluation
